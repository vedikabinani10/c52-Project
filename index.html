Camels

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels can completely shut theur nostrils during sandstorms.
  3. Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth.

Chameleons

  1. Chameleons have eyes at the back of their heads.
  2. Chameleons have panoramic vision.
  3. Unlike many lizards, Chameleons cannot regrow their tails.

Pigeons

  1. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  2. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  3. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

Pandas

  1. An adult panda can eat 12-38 kils of bamboo per day.
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  3. Pandas are good at climbing tress and can also swim.

Cicadas

  1. Cicadas can buzz louder than a lawnmower.
  2. More than 3,000 species of Cicadas exist.
  3. Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.

Teddy Bears

  1. Teddy bears are fluffy.
  2. Teddy bears are available in brown, pink, white and many more colours.
  3. Most children own teddy bears.
